Smashed cucumber salad

Description

This Smashed Asian cucumber salad recipe is one of our most popular recipes for a reason! It’s light, refreshing and easy to make in just 10 minutes.
It’s also packed with flavor from a zingy dressing, garlic, and cilantro.

Ingredients:

  • 1-1.5 pounds seedless cucumbers
  • teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 3 teaspoons light soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2-4 cloves garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1-2 teaspoons chili oil (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame seeds
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro

Steps:

  1. Wash the cucumbers and pat them dry with a clean towel.
  2. Make the salad dressing by combining the salt, sugar, sesame oil, light soy sauce, and rice vinegar.
  3. Stir until the sugar and salt are completely dissolved. Set aside.
  4. On a cutting board, lay a large knife flat against the cucumber, and smash it lightly with your other hand. The cucumber should crack open and smash into four sections. Repeat along its full length.
  5. Once the whole cucumber is completely open, cut it at a 45-degree angle into bite-sized pieces.
  6. In a large bowl, mix the cut cucumber with the prepared dressing, garlic and chili oil. Toss it well. Serve, garnished with sesame seeds and cilantro.
